Description

This classic cheese pizza recipe delivers a perfect balance of crispy crust, tangy tomato sauce, and gooey melted cheese. It's a versatile dish that can be customized with your favorite toppings, making it ideal for a quick weeknight dinner or a fun gathering with friends. The simple preparation ensures a delicious homemade pizza every time.

Our recipe focuses on a thin, chewy crust that crisps up beautifully in the oven, a vibrant sauce made from ripe tomatoes, and a generous layer of mozzarella. Feel free to experiment with additional ingredients like fresh basil, mushrooms, or pepperoni to make it truly your own.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Cheese
  • Topping

Steps

  1. Prepare double
  2. First Rise
  3. Preheat Oven
  4. Shape Dough
  5. Add Toppings
  6. Transfer and Bake
  7. Rest and Serve
